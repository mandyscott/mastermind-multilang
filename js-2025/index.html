How to Play Mastermind

Objective: Guess the secret 4-color code in 16 tries or less!


How to Play:

  • Click on colors to build your guess (4 colors needed)
  • Click "Submit Guess" when ready
  • Use the feedback to improve your next guess

Feedback Pegs:

  • Black peg: Correct color in correct position
  • White peg: Correct color in wrong position
  • No peg: Color not in the code

Note: Feedback pegs don't correspond to specific positions. Black pegs always appear first.
